Dream Baby Dream

A 20-minute collaborative experimental film in which 60 Berlin community members — children, teenagers and adults, professional and amateur artists — use music, movement and poetry to explore home and belonging, dream and utopia, and experiences of trauma.

Winner · COUNTERPOINT Award Liminal Dance Film Festival 2026 · Washington DC Berlin · 2026
