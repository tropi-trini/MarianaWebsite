Creative Producer · Film & Media · Berlin

Mariana Hoyos

Colombian-born, Berlin-based creative producer working across filmmaking, set design, live editing and event production. She began in Bogotá doing art direction on short films with the collective Arte Kaput, moved into streaming as producer and live editor of the noise festival T.A.T.V., and was media manager on Dream Baby Dream. She works across Spanish, English and German.
