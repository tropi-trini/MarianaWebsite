About

Mariana Hoyos is a Colombian-born, Berlin-based creative producer working across live editing, camera, film and event production.

Her practice has shifted over the years: rooted early on in art direction and production design for independent film and installations, it now centres on camera work and — above all — live streaming and real-time editing of concerts, performances and festivals. Whether behind a switcher during a live broadcast or shaping a scene on set, she brings the same instinct: turning raw spaces, teams and ideas into something that holds together and moves people.

She works across Spanish, English and German — mirroring the transnational landscape of the productions she moves through.
